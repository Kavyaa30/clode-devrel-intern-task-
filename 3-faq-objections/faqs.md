# Clode – FAQs

1. Is Clode a replacement for Terraform?
No. Clode works on top of existing infra tools and automates operations.

2. Who is Clode for?
Platform teams, DevOps engineers, and cloud-heavy engineering teams.

3. Does Clode require changing our current stack?
No. It integrates with existing workflows.

4. How is this different from CI/CD tools?
CI/CD handles build and deploy. Clode focuses on operate and monitor.

5. Is this only for large enterprises?
No. Mid-size and scaling teams benefit the most.

6. Is AI making decisions automatically?
AI assists and automates repetitive ops tasks, not critical business logic.

7. How does Clode compare to platform tools?
Clode reduces manual ops instead of adding more configuration.

8. What clouds does Clode support?
Designed for multi-cloud environments.

9. Is there a learning curve?
Minimal — built with developer experience in mind.

10. Why not just build this in-house?
Maintenance cost and operational complexity grow very quickly.
